About Website

Creation

This Website was created for the course Capstone Project by Two Students of the T.A. marryshow Community College Kemayah Noel and Kayden Mitchell who came up with the idea to make a quiz website that can be used by teachers and students to motivate and give student fun optioon to take a quiz.

Kemayah found the design and gave the idea of adding a radial menu for users to get additional things that they may or may not find in a quiz website. The goal of this website was to keep student from dropping out of school and helping them understand their work more.
